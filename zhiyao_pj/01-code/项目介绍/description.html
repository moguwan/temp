项目摘要 (Project Summary)

1. Background / 背景

Emerging epidemiological evidence substantiates the "Oral-Brain Axis," implicating the periodontal pathogen Porphyromonas gingivalis (Pg) in Alzheimer's disease (AD) pathogenesis. Pg establishes recalcitrant biofilms within periodontal pockets, serving as a chronic reservoir for toxic Gingipains. These proteases compromise the blood-brain barrier (BBB) and drive neuroinflammation. Conventional antibiotics exhibit poor penetration into these biofilm matrices. Consequently, persistent bacterial colonization accelerates cognitive decline. We propose a localized, multi-functional engineered biotherapeutic strategy to dismantle the biofilm, eliminate the pathogen, and neutralize toxins, thereby mitigating the risk of neurodegeneration.

新兴的流行病学证据证实了"口-脑轴"的存在，表明牙周病原体牙龈卟啉单胞菌 (Pg) 与阿尔茨海默病 (AD) 的发病机制密切相关。Pg 在牙周袋内建立顽固的生物膜，作为毒性蛋白酶 (Gingipains) 的长期储存库。这些蛋白酶会破坏血脑屏障 (BBB) 并驱动神经炎症。传统的全身抗生素难以穿透这些生物膜基质。因此，持续的细菌定植加速了认知功能的衰退。我们提出一种局部的、多功能的工程化生物治疗策略，旨在瓦解生物膜、清除病原体并中和毒素，从而缓解神经退行性病变的风险。

2. Design of Modules / 模块设计

Chassis Organism: We utilized the clinically validated probiotic Escherichia coli Nissle 1917 (EcN) as our chassis. To ensure therapeutic efficacy, we engineered the strain with the PelB signal peptide. This modification enables the efficient translocation of recombinant enzymes into the periplasmic space and their subsequent release, ensuring direct targeting of the periodontal niche.

Module 1: Biofilm Disruption ("Melt & Cut"): To dismantle the biofilm fortress, we fused Dispersin B (DspB) and DNase I. DspB specifically hydrolyzes poly-N-acetylglucosamine (PNAG), the structural "glue" of the biofilm, while DNase I degrades extracellular DNA (eDNA), the stabilizing "mesh." This synergistic approach collapses the biofilm architecture and exposes the embedded bacteria.

Module 2: Pathogen Elimination ("Kill"): To directly eliminate the pathogen, we integrated Cas9 endonuclease. Upon release and delivery into the target bacteria, Cas9 induces DNA double-strand breaks in the P. gingivalis genome, causing rapid bacterial death and preventing recolonization.

Module 3: Toxin Neutralization ("Block"): To protect the BBB, we incorporated Cystatin C (Cys). As a potent cysteine protease inhibitor, Cystatin C specifically binds to and blocks the active sites of extracellular Gingipains, effectively cutting off the "Oral-Brain" toxic pathway.

Response & Regulation Mechanism: Recognizing the unique microenvironment of deep periodontal pockets, we constructed an anaerobic-inducible expression circuit. The engineered vector utilizes the synthetic nirB promoter, regulated by the host's FNR protein. This design guarantees precise therapeutic release at the hypoxic infection site while minimizing off-target effects in oxygen-rich tissues.

底盘生物：我们选用经临床验证的益生菌大肠杆菌 Nissle 1917 (EcN) 作为底盘。为确保疗效，我们利用 PelB 信号肽 对其进行了改造。这一修饰使重组酶能高效转运至周质空间并随后释放，确保直接靶向牙周病灶。

模块一：生物膜瓦解（"融化+剪切"）：为了瓦解生物膜壁垒，我们融合了 Dispersin B (DspB) 和 DNase I。DspB 特异性水解生物膜的结构"胶水"——多聚N-乙酰葡糖胺 (PNAG)，而 DNase I 降解作为稳定"网格"的胞外 DNA (eDNA)。这种协同策略导致生物膜结构崩塌并暴露内部细菌。

模块二：病原体清除（"杀灭"）：为了直接清除病原体，我们整合了 Cas9 核酸内切酶。Cas9 释放并进入目标细菌后，会在 P. gingivalis 基因组中诱导 DNA 双链断裂，导致细菌迅速死亡并防止再次定植。

模块三：毒素中和（"阻断"）：为了保护血脑屏障，我们纳入了 Cystatin C (Cys)。作为一种强效的半胱氨酸蛋白酶抑制剂，Cystatin C 特异性结合并阻断胞外 Gingipains 的活性位点，有效切断"口-脑"毒性通路。

响应与调控机制：针对深牙周袋独特的微环境，我们构建了 厌氧诱导表达回路。工程化载体利用受宿主 FNR 蛋白调控的合成 nirB 启动子。该设计保证了治疗因子在缺氧感染部位的精准释放，同时最大限度地减少在富氧组织中的脱靶效应。

3. Experiment / 实验

Construction and Validation of the Hypoxia-Inducible Vector

Plasmid Construction:

Vector Engineering: We replaced the T7 promoter of the pET28a backbone with the anaerobic-sensitive nirB promoter to enable hypoxia sensing.

Payload Assembly: A multi-gene expression cassette containing PelB-DNASE1-linker-dspB and Cas9-Cys was synthesized. This multi-domain cassette was cloned into the modified vector between the BglII and NotI restriction sites.

Validation: As shown in the figure, restriction enzyme digestion confirmed the correct insertion of the large DNA fragment. Furthermore, Western Blot analysis of the culture supernatant under anaerobic conditions is expected to reveal distinct bands corresponding to the specific molecular weights of the therapeutic proteins. This confirms that the plasmids were successfully introduced and the therapeutic agents were efficiently secreted by the engineered EcN chassis.

低氧诱导载体的构建与验证

质粒构建：

载体工程：我们将 pET28a 骨架的 T7 启动子替换为对厌氧敏感的 nirB 启动子，以实现低氧感应。

负载组装：合成了包含 PelB-DNASE1-linker-dspB 和 Cas9-Cys 的 多基因表达框。该多结构域片段被克隆至修饰载体的 BglII 和 NotI 限制性酶切位点之间。

验证：如图所示，限制性内切酶酶切确认了融合片段的正确插入。此外，厌氧条件下培养上清液的 Western Blot 分析 预期将显示出与治疗蛋白特异性分子量相对应的清晰条带。这证实了质粒已成功导入，且治疗制剂已被工程化 EcN 底盘高效分泌。

4. Modeling / 建模

Simulation of Biofilm Degradation and Toxin Inhibition

System Dynamics: We utilized Michaelis-Menten kinetics to model the synergistic activity of DspB and DNase I, alongside the inhibitory effect of Cystatin C on Gingipains.

Results:

Fig.1. Biofilm Collapse: The model predicts that our multi-enzyme strategy leads to a non-linear, accelerated collapse of biofilm biomass compared to single-enzyme treatment.

Fig.2. Toxin Neutralization: Simulation indicates that the release of Cystatin C significantly reduces the activity of free Gingipains, theoretically maintaining the integrity of the Blood-Brain Barrier (BBB) below the threshold of neuroinflammation.

生物膜降解与毒素抑制模拟

系统动力学：我们利用 米氏方程 (Michaelis-Menten kinetics) 对 DspB 和 DNase I 的协同活性，以及 Cystatin C 对 Gingipains 的抑制效应进行了建模。

结果：

图1. 生物膜崩解：模型预测，与单酶处理相比，我们的 多酶策略 导致生物膜生物量呈非线性的加速崩解。

图2. 毒素中和：模拟表明，Cystatin C 的释放显著降低了游离 Gingipains 的活性，理论上将血脑屏障 (BBB) 的完整性维持在神经炎症阈值以下。

5. Discussion / 讨论

Comprehensive "Degrade-Kill-Block" Strategy

While traditional therapies struggle with biofilm resistance, our engineered EcN-Multi-Payload system offers a three-tier defense.

Degrade: DspB and DNase I dismantle the biofilm shield.

Kill: Cas9 delivers a lethal blow to the pathogen's genome.

Block: Cystatin C directly neutralizes residual Gingipains (Li et al., 2023), preventing downstream neurodegeneration.

This "Trap-and-Clear" iteration represents a holistic approach to severing the pathogenic link between oral infection and Alzheimer's pathology. Future work will focus on optimizing the delivery efficiency of Cas9 into the target pathogen.

综合"降解-杀灭-阻断"策略

尽管传统疗法难以应对生物膜耐药性，我们工程化的 EcN-多负载 系统提供了三层防御：

降解：DspB 和 DNase I 瓦解生物膜屏障。

杀灭：Cas9 对病原体基因组进行致命打击。

阻断：Cystatin C 直接中和残留的 Gingipains (Li et al., 2023)，防止下游的神经退行性病变。

这种"捕获-清除"的迭代代表了一种切断口腔感染与阿尔茨海默病病理之间联系的整体方法。未来的工作将集中于优化 Cas9 进入目标病原体的递送效率。
